About Me

More about the girl behind the computer!

Aside from academics, I am also very passionate about my hobbies. Most notably, I love to run and I have the most perfect running partner: a 3-year old German Shepherd/Husky Mix. Coming from an animal-background, animals will always be a passion of mine and luckily for Milo, he reaps the benefits and is a very spoiled pup. I also enjoy reading books in my free time. I just recently started learning to crochet. So far, I have mastered the classic dish rag.

Gallup's strength finder concluded my top 5 strengths are achiever, harmony, relator, includer, and positivity. These are all apparent in not only my professional life but also my personal life. There is nothing I love more than to surround myself around friends and family. I have two older sisters and just recently became upgraded to an aunt to the perfect niece! Also, this wouldn't be an about me page if I didn't mention my love for Starbucks, it is absolutely my favorite place to work on my projects.
