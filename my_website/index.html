A Little Bit About Me

I took a coding class my senior spring semester and I haven't been able to stop learning since.

I graduated in 2023 from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Animal Sciences. While earning my Economics minor, I took a class called "Economic Analysis of Data" during my last semester. I had no idea what R meant in the syllabus but I needed the class to earn my minor. Very soon, I found myself having fun completing assignments and working ahead to learn more. Coming from Biochemistry and Organic chemistry, the words "fun" and "assignments" never went together. I decided I wouldn't let the fun stop when that class ended and decided to pick up Python and become fluent in it. I currently hold my AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner certificate and am studying to earn the AWS Certified Solutions Architect certicate next. All the while mastering BOTO3 and diving into linux.
