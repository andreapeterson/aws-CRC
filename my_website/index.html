A Little Bit About Me

My journey into the world of technology began during my senior spring semester when I took a coding class, and since then, I've been on an unstoppable quest for knowledge.

In 2023, I proudly graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Animal Sciences. Along the way, I added an Economics minor to my academic achievements. During my last semester, a class called "Economic Analysis of Data" introduced me to the intriguing world of programming. Despite initially not knowing what "R" meant in the syllabus, I quickly found myself enjoying assignments and proactively diving into the subject.

Coming from a background in Biochemistry and Organic Chemistry, the combination of "fun" and "assignments" was a pleasant surprise. Determined not to let the excitement fade away, I decided to delve into Python, becoming fluent in the language. Currently, I hold the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner certificate and am diligently studying to achieve the AWS Certified Solutions Architect certificate. My journey also involves mastering BOTO3 and exploring the realms of Linux and Terraform.
